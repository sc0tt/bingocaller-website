Bingo Caller

Android Bingo Calling Application with Google Cast Functionality

About

Bingo Caller is an android application available on the Google Play Store which allows you to host bingo nights with ease.

Features

Google Cast

Cast your bingo board to your television to provide all your players with the current list of called balls.

Auto Draw


Bingo Caller can automatically call the next bingo ball at a specified interval.

Multiple Game Types


Bingo Caller supports multiple game types for every type of bingo you want to play.
