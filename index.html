Bingo Caller

Android Bingo Calling Application with Google Cast Functionality

About

Bingo Caller is an android application available on the Google Play Store which allows you to host bingo nights with ease.

Features

  • Casting - Utilize your television to display the bingo board to all players. (This app is Chromecast-enabled)
  • Game Types - Choose from 30, 75, 80, 90, 100, and custom (up to 1000) ball variations.
  • Manual Call - Call random numbers or press and hold any number to manually call a number.
  • Auto Call - Specify a time interval for balls to be called automatically.
  • Announcer - Speak the called numbers through your device and television, multiple voices are available.
  • Patterns - Program patterns that can be played.
  • Exclude - Ignore specific numbers if they are not used in a game.
  • Bingo Lingo - In 90-ball mode enable this option to hear and see the bingo lingo.
  • Call Order - Ascending, Descending, and Random (Most common)
  • Ball History - View all previously called balls.
  • Crazy Call - Call all numbers at the same time ending with a specific number.
